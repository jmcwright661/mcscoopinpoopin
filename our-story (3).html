The story behind McScoopin Poopin

Built by a Dog Owner,
for Dog Owners.

A local business born from a simple idea — and built with everything I had.

Hi, I'm John.

A few years ago I lost my IT job of 5 years. It wasn't how I planned things, but sometimes life pushes you in a direction you didn't see coming.

One afternoon I was out in my backyard picking up after my dog and the thought hit me: "I wish someone would just do this for me." I went inside, sat down at my computer, and built McScoopin Poopin from scratch.

I'm a one-man operation. I handle every yard personally. There's no team, no shortcuts, and no excuses. When I show up, I do a thorough job, sanitize my equipment, and send you a gate photo before I leave.

I started this business in the Antelope Valley because this is my community. Lancaster, Palmdale, Quartz Hill — these are my neighbors. I pour everything I have into every yard I service because this business is everything to me.

If you're tired of spending your weekends dodging dog waste — especially in this AV summer heat — I've got you covered. Let me handle the dirty work.
