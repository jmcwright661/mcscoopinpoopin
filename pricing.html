Pricing FAQ

Does the price change based on yard size?

Base pricing covers most standard yards. Larger yards or properties with multiple fenced areas may require a custom quote. Fill out the form and we'll give you an exact number.

What if I have more than 2 dogs?

Base pricing includes up to 2 dogs. Additional dogs may affect your quote slightly. Let us know in the form and we'll factor it in.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No contracts, ever. You can cancel your recurring service at any time. We keep customers by doing great work, not locking them in.

How does billing work?

Recurring service is billed monthly. We'll discuss payment options when we set up your service.

Is the first week really free?

Yes — every new recurring client gets their first week of service completely free. No catch. That's $25 off your first month.
